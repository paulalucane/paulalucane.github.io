Coffee Table Book

These coffee table book spreads were created as an assignment for the Foundations of Graphic Design course.

I chose Baiba Prindule-Rence as the subject of my spreads. She is a Latvian interior designer and event decorator with an eye for aesthetics. She is a big inspiration to me, and I admire her ability to create magic in any space. You can read more about her in these pages.
