Visual Identity for Knitting Needles

Knitting Needles is an imaginary knitting club for the local community. Its aim is to connect elderly women by providing a sense of purpose, belonging, and a place to socialize, combating the loneliness that this target group often experiences.

Values: Community, purpose, positivity, and time well spent.

This visual identity consists of a logo, poster design, business card, and knitted mittens. The logo is inspired by knitting pattern symbols commonly found in knitting booksâ€”something the elderly audience might recognize.

Note: Since creating this project in 2022, I have learned a lot and would improve the typography and layout of the poster by creating more white space and choosing a more readable, elderly-friendly font.
