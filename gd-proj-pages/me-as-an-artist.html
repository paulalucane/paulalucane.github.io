Me as an Artist Poster

Task: Create a poster that reflects me as an artist. I created this poster to represent all the things that inspire me and my work. Books and stories are one of my greatest inspirations, which is why I used torn book pages to create a backdrop for the photo. I then overlaid images representing travel, music, and movies on top.

I drew inspiration from old billboard designs with light-up letters. Coca-Cola billboards were a significant part of my mood board.

More than anything, as an artist, I often end up with too many, sometimes similar, versions of a single work that I can't choose between. That's why it felt fitting to combine four different versions of the poster in the final product and challenge the viewer to "Find 10 differences," which would help engage the audience.
