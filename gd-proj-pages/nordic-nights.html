Nordic Nights Poster

"The Darkest Nights Bring the Sweetest Skies"

The winter months can be a hard and sad period, especially for those living higher up north. The nights are dark and cold, so are the days. However, cotton candy skies is a wonderful miracle to come out of such darkness.

Created with: Adobe Illustrator

Idea and Concept

Cotton Candy skies is a term often used to describe the pastel skies that occur during sunset or sunrise. What is more, I believe that the winter sunsets and sunrises have a very specific and unique look to them, that cannot be seen during the warmer months. It is especially wonderful to see this phenomenon up north, where the sunrise merges into the sunset, and the only daylight is accompanied by these colors.

I was very inspired after experiencing this myself in Lapland, and wanted to dedicate this poster assignment to the cotton candy skies that only the winter can bring.

Purpose

This poster should serve as an inspirational piece, encouraging people to enjoy and embrace even the darkest months of the year, to enjoy the beautiful winter skies and get the most out of those few hours of light.

Creation

The name of the piece “The Darkest Nights Bring the Sweetest Skies” is connected to the sweetness of cotton candy. The poster is targeted towards a Scandinavian audience, that takes the dark winter months the hardest. I chose the colors to represent the pastels of the sky, and illustrated a fir tree forest clearing and a little cabin in the woods, since it represents the nordic landscape. The poster text “nordic nights, sweet skies” reflects the contrast of the darkness of nordic skies and the sweetness of the cotton candy skies.
