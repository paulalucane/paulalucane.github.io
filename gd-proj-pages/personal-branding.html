Personal Branding

This is my personal branding, created as a part of university assignments.

This is possibly the project I have had the most fun creating. I put in a lot of work to make this truly personal and meaningful in every detail, and it might just be my proudest design so far.

Created with: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Procreate

Welcome to the branding collection of paula lucane designs. This is my personal branding and reflects not only me as a person but me as an artist and designer as well. I have put a lot of consideration from brand values and logo to patterns and their use to ensure that what you see next is as much “me” as possible.

Values: playful, warm, delicate

Colours

I chose the following colors based on what I liked and what I thought fit my personality well. I love autumn, I take a lot of photographs during that time, and often use warm colors in my designs. That is why I chose the warm oranges. Blue complements orange, but I mostly reach for more dusty colors, so I chose less saturated blues. I added pink because it is my favorite color and it adds more playfulness to the mix.

Imagery

Imagery: warm, calm, low to mid exposure, with nature elements, candles, warm drinks where possible. Since this is my personal branding I shall aim to only use photography taken by me or of me.

Motion

Motion can and should be used to engage the viewer. However, it should remain controlled and deliberate, avoiding excessive speed or chaotic movements that could disrupt the brand's calm and inviting atmosphere. It can be playful, while maintaining a sense of delicacy and balance.

Icons

These icons can be used for anything from Instagram highlights to cute stickers to seal shut packaging envelopes.
