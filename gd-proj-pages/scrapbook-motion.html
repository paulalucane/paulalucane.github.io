Scrapbook Advertisement Video

I love travelling and collecting memories from the various places I have explored. Scrapbooking is a great way to collect photos and other keepsake items from such adventures. This is a video to advertise a fictional company and their scrapbooking products.

The project was created as part of content creation course's motion assignment at Jönköping University. Read more about the creation process below!

Created with: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Watch the full video on YouTube!

Idea and Concept

Scrapbooking is a great way to collect photos and other keepsake items from travelling, adventures, or just the small moments of everyday life. This is a video to advertise a fictional company and their scrapbook products.

I wanted to focus on evoking the feelings, values and representing the ideal lifestyle of someone that might buy and use the product instead of focusing on product facts. The concept is surrounded by travel imagery, laughing with friends, real and unscripted situations, joy, happiness, energy, and nostalgia.

Content Creation

To achieve the right look that would reflect the concept I cut out images as if they were inside a scrapbook and used cut-out magazine letters for text. I edited the images so they looked vintage and taken on a film camera. I worked in Adobe After Effects to make them move like independent paper pieces. I chose joyful videos and upbeat music.

I kept the video brief, as there is no deeper narrative underneath that would keep the audience invested for a full length of a longer video. But the visuals are colorful and eye-catching, which will still capture the viewers attention at the beginning and keep them watching for the duration of this ad, while making them feel the way using the product itself (the scrapbook) will make them fee.
