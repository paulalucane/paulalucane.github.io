Light and Shadow Themed Postal Stamps

These postal stamps are inspired by the strong contrast of light and shadow. The task was to create stamps in this theme. I decid to include actual places in the design so a letter can be sent with a postal stamp representing the location.

I used some of my own pictures from travelling for reference. I decided to keep the rough "messiness" from my initial sketches as a design element in my final stamps.

Created with: Procreate, marker on paper
