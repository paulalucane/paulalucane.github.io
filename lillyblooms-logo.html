Lilly Bloom's Flower Shop Logo

This is a logo for a flower shop. The story behind the logo, brand's values, aesthetic: The owner wants it to be different, not pastel and lovely sweet flowers like we are used to. She wants it dark, moody, elegant, villainous.

My created logo is a play on the rose & dagger concept, however, instead of a rose, there is a lilly. What is special about this exact lilly is that it is called "red spider lilly" or "corpse flower", which I thought was very fitting and aligned with the brand values.
