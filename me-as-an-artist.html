Me as an Artist Poster

Task: Create a poster that reflects me as an artist. I created this poster representing all the things that inspire me and my works. Books and stories is one of my greatest inspirations - that is why I used torn out book pages to create a backdrop for the photo. I overlayed images representing traveling, music, movies on top.

I took inspiration from old billboard designs with light-up letters. Coca-cola billboards were a big part of my moodboard.

More than anything, as an artist, I always have too many and often similar versions of one work that I cannot choose between. That is why it felt only fitting to combine 4 different versions of the poster in the final product and tell the viewer to "Find 10 differences", which would help with audience engagement.
