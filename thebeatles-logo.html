New Logo for The Beatles

Task: create a logo for The Beatles if they still existed today.

I decided to combine the iconic Abbey Road pedestrian crossing image with their Let it Be album cover that has four sections. I kept the primary logo simple to fit the modern times. The logo can also use any two colours that feel suitable (I chose red and aquamarine blue). For the final, and possibly more fun, version, the black logo can sit on an illustration or another graphic. The hippie inspired illustrations are the main ones, but could be replaced by something else depending on what is being promoted at the time, like a campaign.
